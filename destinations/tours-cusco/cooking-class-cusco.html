Peruvian Cooking Class in Cusco (Half Day)

Shared Service $69

Half Day
Overview

We are committed to deliver the best experience for our guests, showcasing the cultural diversity of our country through our gastronomy, taking you on a trip across its history and learning about professional cooking techniques in our fully equipped cooking studio where you'll prepare a delicious full meal always guided by our experienced chef.

Highlights

• Learn to prepare authentic Peruvian dishes with hands-on guidance from expert chefs.

• Discover and use unique Peruvian ingredients like quinoa, and native potatoes.

• Master the art of making Peru's iconic cocktail, the Pisco Sour.

• Learn the secrets to making the perfect ceviche, Peru's famous fresh seafood dish.

• Visit a local market to select fresh ingredients and explore the vibrant food culture of Peru.

Itinerary

Day 1: Peruvian Cooking Class in Cusco (Half day) (Altitude: 3400 m. - 11150 ft.)

Be immersed into a fascinating trip to the San Pedro Market, where you’ll be introduced to a vast variety of local and ancient products, learn about the history behind them and their impact in today modern Peruvian gastronomy. Later, we’ll invite you to our cozy cooking studio, located inside a colonial times house, where you’ll step back in time to enjoy a modern Peruvian cooking class guided by a professional Peruvian chef and rounding up all we saw at the market.

During your 4 hour gastronomical experience, we'll provide:

• An advanced english speaking professional chef and guide.

• A fully immersive one tour across the local San Pedro Market.

• A three hours hands-on cooking class in our private Cooking Studio.

• Pisco Sour making class.

• Hands on opportunity to create a three course andean/peruvian inspired meal.

• Exotic fruit tasting dessert.

• All ingredients and equipment.

• Bottled water.

Meals: Lunch or Dinner

Recommendations

• Bring a small notebook to jot down the chef's best tips.
