ABOUT GET TO PERU

Our main office is located in the ancient city of Cusco but our veins run through all parts of Peru, with an extensive network of connections throughout the country. Rest assured we will be there when you need us, whatever part of Peru we will be there.

Personalized Tours

Our staff is always available for you around the clock 24/7. Our personalized tours are designed to leave not just a positive impression, but a lasting one too. You'll go home with outstanding memories, priceless family memories, and a greater understanding of the Peruvian Culture.

Mission

Get To Peru provides authentic, one-in-a-lifetime travel experiences, by taking travelers to local communities and cultural heritage, without negatively effecting the environment or the cultural identity of the places visited. Get To Peru closely cooperates with its suppliers and clients to provide them with guidelines on how to operate and act responsibly, preserving the cultural heritage of each destination.
